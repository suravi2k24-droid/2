Food Stall & Cleanliness Guidelines

1️⃣ Maintain Hygiene Standards – Ensure all cooking and serving areas are clean, and staff follow proper hygiene practices, including wearing gloves and hairnets.

2️⃣ Fresh & Safe Ingredients – Use fresh, high-quality ingredients and store perishable items at the right temperature to prevent contamination.

3️⃣ Proper Waste Disposal – Keep separate bins for wet and dry waste, regularly dispose of garbage, and avoid food spillage to maintain a clean environment.

4️⃣ Sanitize Equipment & Utensils – Regularly clean cooking tools, serving plates, and countertops with food-safe disinfectants to prevent bacterial growth.
