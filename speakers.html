About

The Speaker Sessions are amongst the premier events of SURAVI. Witnessing great stalwarts from varied domains every year, this event serves an inspiration to the young generation and provides a platform for the audience to get inspired from the stories of eminent personalities and imbibe their ideas to walk on their own path of glory.

Dr. Satya Prakash Panda

Dr. Satya Prakash Panda is the founder and chairman of GIET University, Gunupur. An edu-entrepreneur, academician, and philanthropist, he has over 30 years of experience in HRM and has authored multiple books. He established GIET University in 1997, transforming it into a premier institution in Odisha. Beyond education, he actively supports social causes, founding Balaniketan (an orphanage) and Swabalamban (an old age home). His visionary leadership in the Gandhi Group of Institutions has earned him national recognition. At 71, he remains dedicated to education, social service, and innovation.

Er. Rama Narayan Sabat

Er. Rama Narayan Sabat serves as the Vice Chairman of the Venkateswara Educational Trust in Bhubaneswar and holds a key position on the Board of Governors at the Gandhi Institute of Excellent Technocrats (GIET), Ghangapatna, Bhubaneswar. In his message to prospective students, he emphasizes the institution's vibrant environment, characterized by inquisitive students and innovative faculty. He invites students to join GIET in its journey toward becoming a globally renowned institution.

Er. Minakshi Panda

Minakshi Panda serves as the Secretary of GIET Ghangapatna, playing a crucial role in the institution’s administration and development. Her dedication and leadership contribute significantly to the smooth functioning and growth of the institute.

Dr. Subhrajit Pradhan

Dr. Subhrajit Pradhan serves as the Principal of Gandhi Institute of Excellent Technocrats (GIET) in Ghangapatna, Bhubaneswar. He is a member of the Board of Governors at GIET, contributing to the institution's strategic decisions. In his message to students, Dr. Pradhan emphasizes the importance of making informed decisions and highlights GIET's commitment to providing a motivating environment for knowledge assimilation, generation, and dissemination. He has been recognized for his leadership and inspiration within the GIET community.
